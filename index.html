Mule and Friends show support for a local favorite restaurant, The Egg & Us.

Mule Expedition Outfitters and friends of Mule show support for a local restaurant, the Egg & Us.

Due to current restrictions, the hospitality industry is hurting tremendously. We wanted to do our part. So we invited friends and customers of Mule Expedition Outfitters to dust off their expedition vehicle and join us this past Sunday at The Egg & Us restaurant in Issaquah, WA.

Everyone followed COVID guidelines that were put in place by the state. Lots of yummy breakfasts were consumed in overland vehicles, campers, under awnings, and even in tents that morning.

Thank you to all who attended and supported our local restaurant and hopefully, this help start a trend around the state and nation.
